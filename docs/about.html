About Statoberry LLP

Statoberry LLP, founded by a passionate group of statisticians, is redefining the way agricultural research engages with data science. We are a forward-thinking analytics firm with a vision to transform complex datasets into insights that are approachable, meaningful, and true to our name a little bit sweet.

Our journey is deeply rooted in academia and innovation. Statoberry is proudly mentored by the Department of Agricultural Statistics at Kerala Agricultural University (KAU), ensuring a strong foundation in scientific rigor and relevance. We have been incubated under the AgTech Launchpad, a joint initiative of NABARD, KAU, and Western Sydney University, Australia (WSU), which has empowered us to refine our ideas and bring them closer to real-world application. As a recognized startup under the Kerala Startup Mission, we continue to scale our solutions with the guidance, support, and ecosystem provided by the state’s premier innovation framework.

At Statoberry LLP, we blend statistical expertise with cutting-edge technology to build platforms, APIs, and tools that support agricultural research, precision farming, and farmer–consumer connectivity. Our work reflects a single belief: that data science, when made simple and accessible, has the power to transform agriculture and create lasting impact.

Our Mission

We empower researchers, educators, and agricultural professionals with intelligent, user-friendly tools. Through AI-powered platforms and cutting-edge APIs, we simplify analytics so that every decision—from the research lab to the field can be evidence-based and impactful.

Our Vision

Our Vision at Statoberry LLP is to make data science in agriculture simple, accessible, and impactful by transforming complex data into actionable insights. We aspire to empower researchers with intuitive tools, support farmers with secure and transparent platforms, and advance agri-technology through intelligent APIs and precision solutions all while bridging academia and innovation to create a future where agriculture is sustainable, connected, and data-driven.
