what is KIF?

KIF (Kissan Finder) is a highly secure and trusted platform where any farmer can register for free. after registration, the KIF team completes verification. once verified, the farmer receives a login and password and a permanent KIF qr code.

when someone scans this permanent qr code, they can view the farmer‚Äôs verified profile: contact details, photos of the farm, cultivation practices, certifications, and more. for every batch of produce sold, the farmer can easily add batch-specific details (harvest date, variety, inputs used, location, quantity, etc.) so buyers can trace each batch to its origin.

permanent qr code usage: the same qr can be printed on product labels, shared digitally, or displayed in shops and markets. it always points to the farmer‚Äôs KIF profile and the latest batch records.

why use KIF?

  • free, verified identity: farmers join at no cost; profiles are verified by the KIF team to build trust.
  • one qr for life: a permanent code that‚Äôs easy to print on labels, packs, or display boards.
  • batch-level traceability: record details for each batch in minutes; buyers can see what they are purchasing.
  • market visibility: a clean public profile with photos, practices, and certifications boosts reach and credibility.
  • consumer confidence: scanning reveals authentic, up-to-date data from KIF‚Äôs secure database.
  • fair trade and branding: transparent origin stories help justify price and build loyal customers.
  • organization ready: cooperatives, fpos, and ngos can manage and showcase networks of verified farmers.
  • security and control: login-based updates; farmers control what they publish while KIF safeguards data.
